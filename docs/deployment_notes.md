# Deployment Notes

## Prototype Scope

This monitoring station is intended for prototype environmental sensing, education, and experimental compost monitoring.

## Deployment Considerations

- use waterproof probes for internal compost sensing
- protect the Arduino and wiring in a dry enclosure
- avoid leaving breadboards exposed outdoors
- provide strain relief where cables enter or leave the compost system
- expect moisture, corrosion, and mechanical instability in compost environments

## Longer-Term Use

For extended operation, engineers should validate:

- probe durability
- enclosure resilience
- measurement stability over time
- moisture-related sensor degradation
- data consistency across changing compost conditions
